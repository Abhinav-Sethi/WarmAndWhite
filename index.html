About Warm & White

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Northern India, Warm & White began as a dream to create chocolates that celebrate our rich culinary heritage. What started as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a beloved brand known for quality and innovation.

Our founder, inspired by the traditional sweets and spices of the region, began experimenting with chocolate recipes that would capture the essence of Northern Indian flavors while maintaining international quality standards.

Today, we continue this mission, creating chocolates that bridge tradition and modernity, bringing the warmth of Indian hospitality to every bite.
